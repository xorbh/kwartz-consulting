If your team is using AI assistants for day-to-day work — writing, research, coding, analysis — it's worth looking at [Anthropic's Claude AI Team plan](https://claude.com/pricing). We recently evaluated the options and believe the Team plan hits the sweet spot for most small-to-mid-sized teams. Here's our breakdown.

## Claude's Plan Lineup

Before diving into the Team plan specifically, here's how it fits into [Claude's full pricing structure](https://claude.com/pricing):

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Trying Claude out |
| **Pro** | $20/month | Individual power users |
| **Max Expanded** | $100/month | Individuals needing heavy usage + Claude Code |
| **Max Ultimate** | $200/month | Maximum individual usage (20x Pro) |
| **Team Standard** | $20/user/month (annual) | Small-to-mid teams (5–150 users) |
| **Team Premium** | $100/user/month (annual) | Heavy usage team members (5x standard) |
| **Enterprise** | $20/seat + usage at API rates | Larger organisations (20+ users) |

## Team Plan Pricing

- **Standard seat: $20/user/month** (annual) or $25/user/month month-to-month
- **Premium seat: $100/user/month** (annual) or $125/user/month month-to-month — 5x more usage than standard seats
- 5–150 users
- Extra usage available at API rates if you hit your limit

The Team standard seat costs the same as an individual [Pro subscription](https://claude.com/pricing) ($20/month) on an annual plan — but you get significantly more for your money.

## What You Get Over Individual Pro Subscriptions

### More Usage

The [Pro plan](https://claude.com/pricing) gives each user roughly 5x the usage of the free tier, with a 5-hour rolling usage window — hit the limit and you wait. The Team plan's standard seat provides **1.25x more usage per session than Pro**, and usage limits reset weekly (seven days after your session starts) rather than on a rolling window. Each member's usage is independent — one person hitting their cap doesn't affect anyone else.

If someone still hits their limit, the Team plan lets you **purchase extra usage at API rates** — something that simply isn't available on Pro.

For power users who need even more, premium seats at $100/month (annual) offer 6.25x the usage of Pro. You can mix standard and premium seats within the same organisation.

### Centralised Billing

Instead of managing 10+ individual Pro subscriptions with separate invoices and payment methods, the Team plan consolidates everything into **one invoice**. This is cleaner for accounting and easier for whoever manages your subscriptions. You also get an **admin console** for managing team members and settings.

### Data Privacy for Client Work

This is a big one. On the Team plan, there is **no model training on your content by default**. If your team handles sensitive client work — strategy documents, financial data, proprietary processes — this is an important safeguard. The [Enterprise plan](https://claude.com/pricing) takes this further with audit logs, custom data retention controls, and a compliance API.

### Shared Workspace and Collaboration

The Team plan includes shared project folders where team members can collaborate on conversations and prompts. This makes it easier to build on each other's work rather than everyone starting from scratch. You also get a 200,000 token context window — useful for working with large documents.

### Additional Team Features

- Claude Code access
- Cowork — collaborative AI features
- Single sign-on (SSO), domain capture, and just-in-time provisioning
- Admin controls for remote and local connectors
- Connectors for Google Drive, Gmail, Calendar, and GitHub
- Enterprise deployment for the Claude desktop app
- Enterprise search across your organisation
- Integrations with Microsoft 365, Slack, and more

## When You Don't Need Enterprise

Anthropic also offers an [Enterprise plan](https://claude.com/pricing), but it's designed for larger organisations that require features like:

- SCIM provisioning
- Role-based access with fine-grained permissioning
- 500,000 token context window
- Audit logs and compliance API
- Network-level access control and IP allowlisting
- Custom data retention controls
- Google Docs cataloging
- Pay-as-you-go pricing with pooled usage across your org

Enterprise requires a minimum of 20 users. If you're a team of 5–20 people without those requirements, the Team plan covers everything you need. You can always upgrade to Enterprise later as the organisation grows.

## Getting Started

Setting up the Team plan is straightforward — see Anthropic's [getting started guide](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9267247-how-do-i-get-started-with-the-team-plan) for the full walkthrough. A few things to note:

- You'll need a **business email** to create the organisation — consumer domains like Gmail or Yahoo won't work for the initial setup
- If you're upgrading from individual Pro/Max accounts, a **new organisation** is created. Your existing account stays separate and data doesn't transfer between them, so you'll want to cancel any individual paid subscriptions afterwards
- You can switch between your personal and Team accounts from the profile menu

For more details on what's included, see [What is the Team plan?](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9266767-what-is-the-team-plan) on Claude's support site.

## Our Recommendation

For teams that are already paying for individual Pro subscriptions — or considering adopting Claude more broadly — the Team plan is a straightforward upgrade. You get more usage, centralised billing, data privacy, and collaboration features, all for a modest per-user premium.

Start with the annual plan at $20/user/month to lock in the lower rate. Add premium seats only for your heaviest users. Re-evaluate for Enterprise when your team grows past 20 or you need advanced compliance features.
